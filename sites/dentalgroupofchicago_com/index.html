Dental Office in South Loop, Chicago, IL

At Dental Group of Chicago, we’re proud to offer a full scope of general, restorative, and cosmetic dentistry in a modern, welcoming environment. Conveniently located in the heart of the South Loop, our office is easily accessible for patients across the greater Chicagoland area.

What truly sets us apart is our commitment to putting patients first. From the moment you walk through our doors, you’ll be treated with compassion, transparency, and respect. Our team of experienced dental professionals brings years of advanced training and hands-on expertise to every treatment — whether you’re here for a routine cleaning, a smile makeover, or anything in between.

We believe that quality dental care should be within reach for everyone. That’s why we work with a wide range of insurance plans and offer flexible financing options to help make your treatment affordable and stress-free.

Your comfort, your goals, and your oral health are at the center of everything we do. We can’t wait to welcome you to our dental family!

Dental Services

We are dedicated to providing you with the highest standard of personalized dental care using the most up to date and innovative dental technologies available.

Today’s advanced dental treatments and materials can give you a healthier, more complete smile that can last you a lifetime. Our Chicago team can help revitalize and maintain your teeth and gums, giving you the smile you’ve always wanted.

From Invisalign, to dental implants, to teeth whitening, and everything in between, our team of dental experts are here to help you find the best treatment for you. We create a personalized treatment plan for every patient, putting your comfort and care first.

dental implants

Dental Implants provide a foundation for replacement teeth that look, feel and function like natural teeth.

invisalign

Invisalign effectively straightens teeth while using a modern approach that is best suited for an active and social lifestyle.

all on four dental implants

All-on-Four Dental Implants are a great solution for the replacement of multiple teeth, giving immediate improvement to your smile.
