Dr. Miles Moore and his highly trained staff at Memphis Center for Family & Cosmetic Dentistry are committed to giving you the smile you’ve always wanted. Memphis family dentist Dr. Miles Moore provides everything you need to keep your smile beautiful, from standard preventative care and teeth cleanings to tooth colored fillings and dental crowns.

As a cosmetic dentist located in Memphis TN, Dr. Miles Moore also proudly offers Memphis Invisalign, porcelain veneers, teeth whitening, gum contouring, dental crowns, dental bonding, and smile makeovers. Additionally, anyone with dental anxiety can rest assured that Dr. Miles Moore is certified in sedation dentistry and has created a calm, spa-like environment for his practice.

We understand that your smile is connected to your overall wellness and happiness, so we offer you the most advanced dental care in a comfortable setting. Our commitment to a caring approach produces dazzling aesthetics and long-lasting health for your smile!
