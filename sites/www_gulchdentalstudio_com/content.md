### Invisalign Orthodontic Treatment

If the only thing keeping you from a celebrity smile is the thought of braces, step through the doors of Gulch Dental Studio, offering [Invisalign in Nashville, TN](/services/invisalign/). We can create the smile you've been dreaming of using comfortable aligners, the virtually invisible solution for straightening teeth.

Invisalign uses a series of clear aligner trays that gently move your teeth into the correct position, benefitting the health, function, and appearance of your smile.

To ensure the best possible result, our dentists use advanced imaging systems that capture 3D scans of your teeth. Our exacting methods allow us to precisely plot the movement of each tooth for predictable results. We'll even show you a digital preview of your new smile so you can make an informed decision.
