Featured Procedures

Explore the dental services we offer

Same-day Crowns

The advanced CEREC system lets our dentists design and fabricate crowns and bridges in a single appointment. Skip the messy impressions and follow-up appointments so you can get on with your busy schedule.

Emergency Dentistry

A broken or knocked-out tooth is painful enough without having to wait for the dentist's office to open. Fortunately, the team at our Nashville office offers emergency care whenever you need it.

Dental Implants

A missing tooth does more than just have an effect on the appearance of your smile: it can have a significant impact on your oral and overall health, too. The roots of a tooth provide important signals to your jawbone, keeping it strong.

Porcelain Veneers

Veneers are custom-crafted porcelain shells that can conceal the issues compromising your smile, giving it a healthy and symmetrical look. Our dentists can provide you with porcelain veneers to provide you with a brilliant confident smile that with proper care, can last 10 years or more.

SureSmile

The Sure Smile system uses a series of discreet, removable plastic trays custom-fitted to your smile. From improved comfort to easier hygiene, SureSmile offers a number of advantages over braces for patients that qualify.
