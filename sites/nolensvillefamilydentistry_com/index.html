About us

At our office, dentistry is about more than healthy teeth—it’s about caring for people. We believe every patient deserves to feel valued, respected, and truly comfortable in the dental chair. That’s why our team is dedicated to creating an atmosphere where kindness and compassion guide everything we do.

Meet Dr. Lori Adamson

Dr. Adamson grew up in The Montgomery/Prattville Alabama area, and earned Her Degree in Chemistry and Cell Biology from Huntingdon College before completing her dental degree at the University of Alabama at Birmingham in 2008.

With 17 years of experience serving patients in both Tennessee and Alabama—including the last 13 years in Murfreesboro—she is proud to bring her expertise and passion for dentistry to the Nolensville community.

Dr. Adamson is now so excited to connect with families in Nolensville and provide compassionate, high-quality care for patients of all ages.

New Patients

We’re excited to welcome new patients of all ages to our office! During your first visit, we’ll take X-rays and photos, perform a thorough dental exam, and provide a dental hygiene appointment tailored to your needs.

Our team will address any questions or concerns you may have, ensuring you feel comfortable and informed. If you’d like a tour of our office, just let us know—we’d be happy to show you around!
